Royal Family from the 1920s till Now

The royal family has undergone many changes and developments since the 1920s. Here is a brief overview:

1920s - 1950s

During this period, notable members of the royal family included King George V, Queen Mary, and their children, including the future King George VI and the Duke of Windsor.

1950s - 1980s

Queen Elizabeth II ascended to the throne in 1952. She married Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh, and they had four children: Charles, Anne, Andrew, and Edward.

1980s - Present

Prince Charles, the eldest son of Queen Elizabeth II, married Lady Diana Spencer in 1981. They had two sons, William and Harry. Prince Charles later married Camilla Parker Bowles.

Prince William, the Duke of Cambridge, married Catherine Middleton in 2011. They have three children: George, Charlotte, and Louis. Prince Harry, the Duke of Sussex, married Meghan Markle in 2018. They have two children: Archie and Lilibet.
